Step 1: App ID
Go to the Facebook Developer’s Quickstart Page and click “Skip and Create App ID”
at the top right. Then create a new Facebook App for your bot and give your app a
name, category and contact email.

Step 2: Choose messenger
You’ll see your new App ID at the top right on the next page. Scroll down and click
“Get Started” next to Messenger.

Step 3: Generate a Page Access Token
Go to Messenger settings for your Facebook App. Using the Page you created
earlier (or an existing Page), click through the auth flow and you’ll receive a
Page Access Token for your app.
